Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you work with clients across Canada?

Yes. We're fully online and serve all provinces and territories. Many of our clients are in British Columbia, but we work with Canadians everywhere.

How does the process work?

You share your documents securely online, we prepare your return (or file your GST/HST, incorporation, etc.), and we keep you updated at every step. No in-person visits required.

How long until I get my refund?

Refunds typically arrive within 2 weeks after filing, depending on CRA processing. Filing early in the season often means faster refunds.

What if I'm self-employed or drive for Uber/DoorDash?

We specialize in self-employed and gig workers. We'll help you report income, claim expenses, and handle GST/HST if you're registered.
